Which Dog Is Right For You?

Dogs come in many shapes and sizes, but also range widely in their personalities. To view full profiles of all dog breeds and learn more about which dog may be right for you, the American Kennel Club's website is an excellent resource. Additionally, the AKC offers a Quiz to match prospective dog owners with dog breeds most suitable to their lifestyles.
