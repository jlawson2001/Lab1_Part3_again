Why Adoption Is A Good Option

Each year, many dogs are unethically bred in puppy mills or are abused by owners and sent away. Animal shelters provide transitional care to many wonderful dogs who are in great need of a home. If you or someone you know are considering getting a dog, we urge you to consider adoption, and to think of all the sweet, loving pups who would love to make you their forever family. A dog can become a man's best friend, and a shelter dog may just become yours.

For prospective dog owners in the greater Boston area, consider shelters such as the MSCPA at Nevin's Farm , the Great Dog Rescue New England or the Northeast Animal Shelter.
