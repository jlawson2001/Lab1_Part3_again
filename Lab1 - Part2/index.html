About Dogs

Dogs are great pets and companions, and can bring joy to you and your family. Owning a dog is a big responsibility, but there are many shelter dogs out there in need of loving homes. If you are interested in learning more about being a dog owner or are considering adopting a dog, you've come to the right place.

Caring For Dogs

In order to maintain a healthy lifestyle, dogs require lots of care and attention. As a dog owner, you should make sure you feed your dog a healthy diet and walk your dog daily or take it to the park to exercise. Dogs are very social, so you will need to spend time playing with your dog each day. For busier dog owners, mobile applications like Wag! and Rover can help you find dogwalkers and petsitters to make sure your dog is taken care of while you are away. However, dogs need stability. If you travel often or have a busy schedule, weigh your options and consider whether being a dog owner is right for you.
